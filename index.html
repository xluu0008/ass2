Top 5 countries with the most Vaccinations September 2021

The top five countries with the most cumulative vaccinations are China, India, United States, Brazil and the United Kingdom.

The total number of vaccinations is the cumulative total of doses or people receiving vaccinations.

By clicking the country name on the legend, you can see the tendency of the total vaccinations for each of the country.

Share of the population fully vaccinated against COVID-19 September 2021

The normalized bar chart at the left-hand side shows the share of the population fully vaccinated for the top ten countries. The dot chart at the right indicates the total vaccinations adopted by the ten countries.

The share of the population fully vaccinated indicates the progress of achieving the goal of COVID-19 immunity and we can understand how much of the population is immune in each country. However, the countries with a higher share of the population fully vaccinated do not always have a higher fully vaccinated rate. By comparing the fully vaccinated rate and total vaccinations adopted, the vaccination efficiency for each country can be evaluated.
